Feh with Thunar

Created Sunday 05 July 2020


Right-click on an image on PCmanFM and set it to open with:
feh --start-at %f


After that you may press space, right cursor or n to see next image (f for fullscreen).


To open icons with Feh on PCmanFM (.svg), right-click on an image and set it to open with:
feh --conversion-timeout 2 --start-at
After that you may press space, right cursor or n to see next image (f for fullscreen).


If that works badly, install:
geeqie


Feh doesn't like gifs, but you can use gifview (gifsicle) on Thunar with:
gifview --animate %f
